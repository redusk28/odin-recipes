Classic Waffles

Fluffy and Fresh Classic Waffles

Serve this lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning.

Ingredient List

  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 4 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 2 Tbsp White Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 Cups Warm Milk
  • 1/3 Cup of Melted Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature./li>
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
